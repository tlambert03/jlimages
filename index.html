About

Joseph Lambert Images is an image-driven branding service for small to medium-sized businesses looking to connect more directly with their clients. If your product or service can be expressed visually, we can help you tell that story. Through professionally captured images, we establish your business concept, content, and intention — and present it through your website and personalized promotional materials. In today's competitive markets, your website is your strongest statement, reaching more clients more directly than almost any other channel. It should be visually worthy of what you offer.

After many years and numerous assignments, everything I've seen and done comes back to the same thing: the power of a captured image to describe a project or present a service. As a professional Connecticut photographer, my goal is to capture for you an event, a still, a portrait, a product, or a finished project. My work includes branding for restaurants, delis, builders, manufacturers, and golf courses. Whether it's a studio portrait or a location shoot, a wedding or corporate event, a boardroom group portrait or a plated entrée — whatever your occasion or imaging need, we can accommodate. The images on this site are here to show our versatility and range. But ultimately, this isn't about us — it's about you and what you need.

Photography today is uniquely personal and individually expressive, and our goal is to present your vision for your purpose. Our design group can produce websites driven by images unique to your product or service — whether you're reaching a local market or a national one. Contact us for a consultation and learn how affordable and helpful our service can be.
